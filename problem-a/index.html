The Man, the Myth, the Legend: Barry B. Benson

The character, Barry B. Benson,is from the award winning animated film, the 'Bee Movie'. The film, produced by Dreamworks Animation, came out in 2007 and quickly left fans with a love and appreciation for its characters and messages. The plot of the film surrounds Barry B. Benson, as he navigates his post-grad experience and searches within himself through his existential crisis. He breaks the rule to never communicate with humans, but discovers through his rebellion the real mistreatment of his species. He dedicates his mission in life to obtain justice for his kind by suing humanity!

Why I Love Barry:

  • He stands for what he believes in no matter what other people think of him.
  • He speaks out for the faults of humanity.
  • He has killer eyebrows.
  • He likes jazz.
